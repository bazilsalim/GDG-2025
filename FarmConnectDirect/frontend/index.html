Welcome to FarmConnect Direct

FarmConnect Direct is a free online platform that connects farmers directly with local buyers. By removing middlemen, we help farmers earn fair prices and buyers get fresh, affordable produce. Farmers can list their products with images and pricing, while buyers can browse by state and district, then contact farmers directly. We focus on local selling to support rural communities and reduce extra costs.
